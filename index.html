31–60 days — Publishing Engine

Outcome: Thought‑leadership cadence + executive LinkedIn voice; six‑week editorial roadmap.

61–90 days — Authority & Scale

Outcome: Anchor op‑ed/panel; quantified case‑proof; crisis drill (FR/AR).
